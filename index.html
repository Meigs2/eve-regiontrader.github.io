RegionTrader


What is RegionTrader?

RegionTrader is an application designed to optimize station trading and region trading in EVE Online.

What can RegionTrader do?

RegionTrader can:
- Find margins for station trading and region trading
- Provide price forecasts
- Plot optimal routes between systems, factoring in gatecamps and warp time
- Provide advanced gatecamp information

How do I use it?

As you can imagine, creating and maintaining something like this takes a lot of time.
For this reason, a payment of 50,000,000 ISK to the character Andre Cadel is required
to use the service.

After sending 50,000,000 ISK, wait about 15 minutes for the next database refresh.
You can then enter your character name below (SSO isn't needed, this application doesn't use scopes).
You will then be redirected to RegionTrader.
